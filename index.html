Our History

Over 40 Years of Service

Shayamabvudzi Primary School was established in 1983 to serve the Mwenezi West community. What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to heritage-driven education, ensuring our students grow up with a deep sense of patriotism and cultural integrity.

Mission

Providing relevant, inclusive, and quality education to empower useful citizens.

Vision

Empowering learners through heritage-based performance in all learning areas.

Academic Structure

Our Specialised Departments

Infantry (ECD A & B)

Where the journey begins. We focus on early numeracy and social development in a safe, fun environment.

Junior Department

Grades 3 to 7 focusing on the core curriculum, literacy, and rigorous exam preparation.

Sports & PE

From soccer to athletics, we ensure our learners develop physical health and sportsmanship.

Heritage & Culture

Dedicated to traditional arts, music, and Zimbabwean history to foster patriotic values.

Home Economics

Practical life skills including nutrition, clothing, and textiles for self-reliance.

ICT & Technology

Bridging the digital divide by teaching modern computer skills to every primary learner.
